Instructions

This is a pomodoro timer that plays a focus video of your choice for 25 minutes, then a break video of your choice for 5 minutes. Input two YouTube URLs, then click start!

Background

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo, where you work for 25 minutes than take a break for 5 minutes. Every third break will be a long break, lasting 15 minutes.
